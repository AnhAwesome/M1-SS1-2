Me, Toys and Games

My room has always been more than just a place to sleep; it's a gallery of miniature worlds and digital adventures. My passion for toys and games didn't start with just "playing" it started with the curiosity of how things work. I remember spent hours meticulously assembling Lego sets, feeling the satisfying "click" of every brick, and later, navigating complex strategy games that challenged my split-second decision-making. These weren't just distractions; they were my first lessons in patience, logic, and creativity. While others saw plastic figures and flickering screens, I saw stories waiting to be told and problems waiting to be solved. Today, that passion has grown into a deep interest in game design and storytelling, proving that the "toys" of my childhood were actually the building blocks of my future career.
